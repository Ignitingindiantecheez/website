Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal

Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research (IISER) are a group of premier public research institutions in India. These institutes were established by the Government of India through the Ministry of Human Resource Development to promote quality education and research in science and related areas. All IISERs were declared as Institutes of National Importance by the Parliament of India in 2012, to promote them as leading institutions in the country in the field of basic sciences. Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal is an autonomous public university in Bhopal district, Madhya Pradesh, India. It was established in 2008 in order to incorporate research in basic science at undergraduate and graduate level, with equal emphasis on higher education for research and education in science. The prime focus of the Institute is to integrate science education with research.

Departments

In the natural sciences stream, the institute has the following departments:

  1. Biological Sciences :- The aim of this department is to foster knowledge in students pertaining to basic and advanced areas of biological sciences by not only pursuing both teaching and high-quality research but also through integrating biology with physics, chemistry and mathematics.

  2. Chemistry :- Research groups in this department are dedicated to carrying out cutting edge research in basic and applied fields of chemistry including materials science, chemical biology, organic and organometallic chemistry, theoretical chemistry, spectroscopy and crystallography.

  3. Earth and Environmental Sciences :- Earth is a dynamic planet whose surface is constantly modified by wind, water, earthquakes, volcanoes, and, increasingly, human activities. The dedicated faculty of this department are engaged in research that is crucial for better understanding our planet.

  4. Mathematics :- It is the constant endeavour of the Mathematics Department to conduct fundamental research in mathematics, and to train students by providing them a broad and solid foundation in Mathematics. It strives to give a balanced training to students, and prepare them for pursuing a research career in mathematics, as well as for a non-academic employment after graduation.

  5. Physics :- The Physics department provides students with the opportunity to explore and nurture interdisciplinary interests by allowing them to take courses in other disciplines along with physics.

In Engineering Sciences stream, the institute offers programmes in:

  1. Chemical Engineering
  2. Data Science Engineering
  3. Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

Under the Humanities and Social Science Stream, one can pursue courses in:

  1. Economic Sciences :- The Department of Economic Sciences at IISER Bhopal was set up in 2017 with the objective of promoting research and training in all areas of Economics. The research interests of the current faculty span microeconomics, game theory, trade theory, industrial organisation, public economics, social choice theory, corporate finance, option pricing, behavioural economics, agriculture economics and environmental economics.
  2. Humanities and Social Sciences :- The Humanities and Social Sciences (HSS) Department at IISER Bhopal was set up recently with the aim of encouraging and inculcating a humanistic approach and awareness among students and researchers of science. It seeks to encourage students to view science holistically as a domain of human knowledge which is not in isolation from, but in productive dialogue with society and culture, and with other areas of human production and thought like creative arts, philosophy and literature. The department has been conceptualised with this vision of foregrounding the interfaces between science, society and culture, and of initiating a dialogue between the natural sciences, the social sciences, and the humanities.
